Spiderman

Marvel's Spider-Man is a 2018 action-adventure game developed by Insomniac Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man, it tells an original narrative that is inspired by the long-running comic book mythology, while also drawing from various adaptations in other media. In the main story, the super-human crime lord Mister Negative orchestrates a plot to seize control of New York City's criminal underworld. When Mister Negative threatens to release a deadly virus, Spider-Man must confront him and protect the city while dealing with the personal problems of his civilian persona, Peter Parker.

Elden Ring

Elden Ring is set in the Lands Between, a realm governed by the Elden Ring, a divine artifact created by the Greater Will, one of the Outer Gods. Marika, chosen as the Elden Ring's vessel and elevated to godhood, formed the Golden Order alongside two Elden Lords: Godfrey, a warrior banished after losing grace, and Radagon, Marikaâ€™s male alter-ego. Marika planted the Erdtree, removed the Rune of Death to prevent true death, and persecuted races like the horned Omens and the Tarnished, warriors stripped of grace. After her son Godwyn's soul was stolen using the Rune of Death, Marika shattered the Elden Ring, leading to her imprisonment and a war among her demigod descendants for its shards. These include Morgott, loyal to the Golden Order; Mohg, who seeks his own dynasty; Radahn and Malenia, cursed twins; Rykard, who merged with a serpent god; Ranni, who opposes the Greater Will; and others, each with unique ambitions. Meanwhile, the Tarnished, exiled warriors resurrected by a mysterious power, are called back to restore the Elden Ring and ascend as the new Elden Lord. In the Shadow of the Erdtree expansion, Marika's origins in the hidden Land of Shadow are revealed, including her people's destruction by the Hornsent and her rise to godhood through the Gate of Divinity.
